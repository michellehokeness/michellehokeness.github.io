Meet our stylists

Michelle Weaver

Hi, I’m Michelle! I graduated from Chico State with a degree in hospitality and event planning. I knew that Salon Sol would be a perfect fit; it allows me to work directly with guests to ensure they receive exceptional customer service and to inspire and encourage all employees to reach their dreams! Can’t wait to see you in the salon!

Alex Smith

Hi, I’m Alex! I started my hair journey well before I got licensed, but made it official in 2009. I love this industry, and helping people feel beautiful inside and out. Color that is my "thing" and my mantra is, “less is more”. When I'm not in the salon, I love going to my Pilates studio and playing board games with friends.

Omar Lutz

Hi, I’m Omar. I have been in the industry since 2015. I am passionate about being an artist with hair and making people feel amazing. I love haircutting more than anything, and love doing creative color/balayage. I’m an adrenaline junkie and love doing spontaneous and crazy stuff sometimes.

Tilly Nguyen

My name is Tilly. I started in the business in 2012. I truly believe everyone is beautiful and I love bringing the beauty out in others. I want the absolute best for humanity. On my time off, I enjoy a tall glass of wine and enjoy it with my four-legged son Charlie.

Fred Steeves

Hi, I’m Fred. I have been part of the Beauty Industry for over a decade. Finishing Beauty School I entered a competitive training program in Santa Monica and Beverly Hills. I also spent 3 years, assisting and training under Wella stylists and educators. I came back to San Diego where I find much inspiration in the looks and trends that I bring to my guests.

Samson McCall

My passion lies mostly with color. I’m ready to give my clients the hair of their dreams.

Kelly Curtis

Hi I’m Kelly, and I started doing hair in 2000. I feel like the thing I’m most passionate about is short haircuts and fantasy colors! Love love love those two things. I’m a big family girl and I’m in love with my fur baby Mikey. My go-to products are Light Moroccan Oil and Enjoy Texture Cream.

Vanesa Anderson

Hello, I’m Vanesa! I began my career in the hair industry in 2015 while living in my home city of Los Angeles, California. Franky Gellar

Phillip Lopez
