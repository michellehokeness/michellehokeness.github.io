Welcome to Salon Sol

Salon Sol is a full-service salon located in the heart of Qualcomm in Sorrento Valley, San Diego. We offer a wide array of services that include haircuts, color, facials, and so much more. Our team is very skilled and has the experience necessary for all of your beauty needs. Our philosophy here is to create an environment where people from all walks of life - YOU - can step away from life’s daily demands and put your trust in the hands of our skilled designers and leave feeling that we’ve really made a difference in your life and in your appearance. Outstanding service is so important to us. Our friendly and eager team is here to help you with achieving your beauty goals. Give us a call or schedule online, we look forward to hearing from you.

Jenny Ta
Salon Sol 2018
Hairstylist/Cosmetologist
Since 2001

Jenny is the owner and creative visionary of Salon Sol. Jenny grew up in sunny San Diego and loves its laid back lifestyle. Schooled in both classic Vidal Sassoon techniques and modern razor cutting, Jenny brings her urban cool training and mixes it with easy, effortless SoCal vibes. She really creates magic for her wide variety of clients.
Jenny brings 20+ years of experience in the industry to Salon Sol, which she acquired in 2018. “I wanted to open my first salon and offer an elevated client experience done in a beautiful space. A place where craft stylists dedicated to growth can pass on inspiration to their clients in a fun and welcoming way.”
